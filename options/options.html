Codeforces PC² Interface

v1.0.0  Codeforces Edition

A custom browser extension that replaces Codeforces contest pages with a faithful emulation of the classic PC² (Programming Contest Control) team interface — complete with submission, verdict polling, clarifications, and live countdown.

Developer: Abdo Sleem
